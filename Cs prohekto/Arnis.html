Kali Arnis

Kali Arnis, also known as Eskrima, is a a martial arts from Philippines that focus in weapon fighting using blunt, improvised and bladed weapons such as sticks, machetes, knives, and batons. This is also the Philippines national martial art and is the first martial art observed in the Philippines. The martial art also includes other techniques such as hand-to-hand combat, joint-locks, grappling, and disarment

Origins

The word Kali comes from the pre-Hispanic word 'Kalis' meaning blades. The word Arnis comes from the word 'arnes' meaning armor but it was also interchangeably used to mean weapon. And lastly, Eskrima is from the Spanish word 'esgrima' meaning fencing which also cognates with the French word 'escrime' meaning skirmish.

Kali Arnis is the ancient way our ancestors, specifically "Pintados", were fighting, and apparently it was so effective that the only thing that stopped them is firearms.

Grading System

There six rank: white, yellow, green, blue, brown, black. And black is divided to 10 ranks.
