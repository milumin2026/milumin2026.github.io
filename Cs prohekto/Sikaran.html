Sikaran

Sikaran is a Filipino martial art that usee hand and mostly foot fighting.

Origins

Sikaran comes from the word "sikad" which means kick in Tagalog, Capampangan, and Cebuano.

Sikaran came from the town of Baras in the Province of Rizal. It is stated to have existed before the Spaniards came into the Philippines. This martial has no written records like other martial passed down orally as such this martial was almost forgotten. It is now currently modernised to be like karate having belt system and uniforms to appeal to the general public.
